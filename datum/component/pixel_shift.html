pixel_shift
/datum/component/pixel_shift

Pixel shift component, applied to any mob/living. Allows the user to shift around their pixel_x and pixel_y offset. This component tracks their shifting and updates their offsets as they pixelshift.

Vars

living_parentThe parent, typecasted to mob/living.
x_shiftOur shift on the X axis.
y_shiftOur shift on the Y axis.

Procs

reset_offsetsResets the user's x and y shift to their base_pixel (+ any position offset)
shift_neg_xShifts the user in the negative X direction (Left)
shift_neg_yShifts the user in the negative Y direction (Down)
shift_pos_xShifts the user in the positive X direction (Right)
shift_pos_yShifts the user in the positive y direction (up)
stop_pixel_shiftResets the user's pixel offsets and qdel's the component
update_offsetsActually update the user's pixel offsets based on our shifts
